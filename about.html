Let me introduce myself, my name is Yusti Mangopang. I was born in Tana Toraja,March 12, 2005. And this year I am 19 years old. My father works as a farm laborer, and my mother is a housewife, I am the fifth of six children, I have two older brothers, two older sisters and one younger brother. My hobbies are drawing, reading novels and singing. I like brown, black and white. For education, I attended elementary school 140 pa'tengko, junior high school 3 Mengkendek, senior high school 3 Tana Toraja, and studied at the Manado State Polytechnic until now.As for my own experience, I feel that it is still lacking because I am not an active child in organizations. When I was in high school I only joined the student council organization once, and the rest I focused on my studies and took part in several extracurriculars held at my high school.

I am a native of the Toraja tribe, South Sulawesi. who decided to migrate to the city of Manado, North Sulawesi and study at the Manado State Polytechnic, I majored in electrical engineering, informatics engineering study program. The reason I decided to study in Manado with a major in informatics engineering, I hope to be able to train myself, train my skills and soft skills so that in the future I can compete in an increasingly difficult world of work. and make me proud of the people who have supported me all this time. that's a small story about myself, thank you.
